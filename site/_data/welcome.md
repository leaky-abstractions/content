# Leaky Abstractions

*from theory to assembly.*

Every abstraction leaks. The interesting work happens at the seams: </br>
where high-level patterns meet runtime, where algorithms meet hardware, </br>
where carefully-designed code meets the messy reality.

Topics span **software engineering**, **architecture**, </br>
and **computer science**, applied through **game dev**, </br>
**embedded systems**, and **systems programming**.

If you've ever wondered *why* something works, </br>
not just *how* to use it — you're in the right place.
